# Free Social Promotion Plan

## Objective

Drive the first 500 qualified visitors to `https://dont-send-it-yet.onrender.com` without paid ads, then learn which message attracts users who actually start a cooldown or save a draft.

## Core Angle

Do not sell this as an AI relationship coach. Sell it as a tiny emergency brake:

> A 10-minute pause for texts you might regret tomorrow.

## Channel Priority

1. TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
   - Best for emotional, urgent, late-night scenarios.
   - Use text-on-screen demos. No need to show face.
2. Reddit
   - Best for learning and selective, non-spam distribution.
   - Comment helpfully first; link only when directly useful and allowed.
3. Pinterest
   - Best for evergreen breakup/no-contact discovery.
   - Use quote-style pins and checklist pins.
4. X / Threads
   - Best for building in public and short emotional hooks.
5. LinkedIn / Indie Hackers
   - Best for product story, not user acquisition.

## 30-Day Execution Rhythm

- Daily: 1 short video reposted to TikTok, Reels, Shorts.
- Daily: 1 text post on X/Threads.
- 3x/week: 1 Pinterest pin.
- 3x/week: 2 sincere Reddit comments, no link unless highly relevant.
- Weekly: 1 build-in-public post on LinkedIn or Indie Hackers.

## Guardrails

- Do not claim therapy, treatment, diagnosis, or mental-health outcomes.
- Do not post in support communities with hard promotion.
- Use the product as a pause ritual, not a replacement for people or professional help.
- Avoid manipulative heartbreak copy. The tone should be calm, not predatory.

## Metrics To Track

- Link clicks by platform.
- `score_generated`
- `cooldown_started`
- `decision_vault`
- `challenge_started`
- Feedback notes containing "helped", "confusing", "too much", or "missing".
