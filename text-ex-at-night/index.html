Night urge

Night can make your ex feel closer than they are.

When the room gets quiet, the brain starts offering old contact as comfort.

A late-night text can turn a temporary wave into a new waiting room. If you are hoping one reply will settle your body, pause before handing them that much power.

Let the wave pass

Use the timer, listen to the background sound, and decide after the first spike has softened. You do not have to solve the whole breakup tonight.
