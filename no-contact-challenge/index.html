No-contact reset

No contact is easier with a pause ritual.

The urge to text often comes in waves. A streak gives you evidence that you can survive the wave without reopening the loop.

A no-contact challenge is not about becoming cold. It is about not letting panic, loneliness, guilt, or habit make contact for you.

What to do when the urge spikes

Paste the message you want to send, check the risk, start the cooldown, and save the message to the vault if you are not ready to delete it.

You can also use the daily check-in to record the trigger and protect your streak one day at a time.

When the streak feels fragile

Use the urge timer for the spike itself, or save the text privately if deleting it feels too hard tonight.
