Should I text my ex?

If you are asking this while anxious, lonely, guilty, or angry, wait ten minutes before sending anything.

The first question is not whether the text is perfectly written. The first question is what state you are sending from. If silence would make you spiral, the message is probably asking for reassurance, not closure.

Use this quick check

Ask yourself what you hope they will say back, what you will feel if they do not reply, and whether the message can wait until your body is calmer.

Don't Send It Yet gives you a regret score, a 10-minute cooldown, and a private vault for messages that need somewhere to go besides their phone.
