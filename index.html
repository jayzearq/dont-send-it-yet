Before you send

Questions people ask at the worst possible hour

Should I text my ex?

If the urge feels intense, pause first. Don't Send It Yet helps you check the regret risk, wait ten minutes, and choose whether to save, delete, or send the draft from a calmer state.

What is a no-contact challenge?

A no-contact challenge is a structured reset period where you avoid contacting an ex, track urges, and protect your streak while your emotions settle.

What should I do with the message instead?

Put it in the vault, write one honest line about what you wanted back, and reread it after the cooldown. A message can be real without needing to become a notification.
