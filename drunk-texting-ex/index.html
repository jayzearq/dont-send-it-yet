Late-night pause

Drunk texting your ex can wait ten minutes.

If the message feels urgent after drinking, it is probably asking for relief more than clarity.

Alcohol lowers the part of you that remembers tomorrow. The text may feel honest, but honesty still deserves timing, steadiness, and a body that can handle no reply.

Before you send it

Paste the draft, check the regret score, and let the cooldown run. If the message still matters in the morning, you can revisit it with a clearer nervous system.
